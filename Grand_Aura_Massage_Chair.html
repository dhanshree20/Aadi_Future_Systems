Grand Aura Massage Chair

The Grand Aura Massage Chair by Osaki (often branded as OS‑Pro Admiral) combines modern therapeutic functionality with a sleek, space-efficient design. It uses a 3D roller mechanism on an L-track to deliver deep tissue massage from the neck down to the upper hamstrings, while automated body scanning personalizes each session. Designed for compact homes, the chair requires only a minimal gap (≈ 2 inches/5 cm) from the wall when reclining. Featuring zero-gravity positions, full-body air compression, foot reflexology rollers, heat therapy, ambient lighting, USB charging, and built-in Bluetooth audio, it offers a luxurious, immersive massage experience across multiple auto programs and manual massage styles. The chair supports tall users (up to ~6′3″/˜190 cm) and comes with a three-year limited warranty covering parts and labor.
