Infinity Relax Zero Gravity Chair

The Infinity Relax Zero Gravity Chair is a full‑body wellness recliner designed to simulate the weightless posture developed by NASA for astronauts. By reclining at approximately 120–128° with the legs elevated at or above heart level, this chair achieves a neutral body posture that evenly distributes body weight. The result: reduced spinal pressure, enhanced circulation, and a deeply relaxing massage experience. Combined with advanced technologies like body scanning, air compression, heat therapy, and multi‑axis roller systems, it delivers personalized therapeutic benefits tailored to your body and needs.
