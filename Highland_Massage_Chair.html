Highland Massage Chair

The Highland Massage Chair (Arama Asia model) is a high-end, full-body massage recliner engineered for complete comfort and advanced therapeutic performance. Featuring an intelligent 3D massage mechanism that simulates human touch, its ultra-long S+L track spans approximately 137 cm—from neck to lower buttocks—allowing deep and comprehensive spinal massage. Designed with space-saving technology, the chair slides forward just 5 cm from the wall when reclining, making it practical for modern home layouts. It offers zero gravity positioning to reduce spinal pressure and enhance circulation, and full-body compression via 38 queen-sized airbags. Additional amenities include heating therapy, automatic footrest extension, a large 7″ touch‑screen tablet, AI voice control, built-in wireless charging and USB ports, plus high-fidelity stereo speakers for a serene massage experience. Users can choose from 23 automated massage programs with quick-access buttons and enjoy safety features like short-circuit protection.
